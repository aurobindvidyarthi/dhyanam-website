The objective

To conduct a long-term study on how daily, silent group breathing affects the brain and body. We aim to measure its impact on stress regulation, focus, and the sense of shared connection.

Why join
the laboratory?

Participation is an engagement in a controlled, distributed experiment investigating collective neurocognitive synchronization.

  • Autonomic Modulation

    Systematically modulate your autonomic nervous system through precise, universal breathing patterns (4:6 ratio).

  • Global Synchronization

    Become a node in a global cohort executing an identical protocol simultaneously.

  • Scientific Contribution

    Contribute to rigorous research on how coordinated respiration influences consciousness and social connection.
